[8.31.2024]

why should you care about my philosophy? listen up, because lately, i've started to believe in making things that work. no bullshit. and if it works, don't add 16 javascript frameworks to it. accessibility isn't just a word, it's fucking important. if your site loads slower than a snail on ganja, you're doing it wrong. performance matters.

i'm not channeling some go minimal bullshit here. but this approach fucking works. it's honest, it's gritty, it's in-your-face. so either get on board or get out of the way.

life. it's all about good people, better friends, and those nights of fucking madness. life gets pretty damn good when you strip away the bullshit and focus on what matters. there's this japanese concept called wabi-sabi - finding beauty in imperfection. that's my fucking jam. your code, your life, doesn't need to be perfect. it needs to work, to have character, to be real. embrace the chaos.

it might sound crazy, but fuck it, it's honest. and sometimes, that's all that matters.
