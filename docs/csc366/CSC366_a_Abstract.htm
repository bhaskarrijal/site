Project Title: A Case Study on Implementation and Impact of E-Nagarpalika

Group: Bhaskar Rijal (27023/077)[1]

 

Abstract: Information technology is reshaping public administration, with e-government initiatives promising to transform governance. This case study examines E-NagarPalika, an innovative e-governance project in Nepal aimed at revolutionizing local government administration. While early e-governance efforts often merely digitized existing processes, E-NagarPalika seeks to leverage advanced ICT mechanisms to create a more efficient, transparent, and citizen-centric model of municipal governance. Our analysis explores the project's implementation, its impact on service delivery, and the challenges faced in transitioning to digital governance at the local level in Nepal. Through this examination, we aim to contribute to the understanding of e-government's potential to improve public administration and citizen engagement in developing nations.
